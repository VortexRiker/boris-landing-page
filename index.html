Why become a cat?

  • You will obtain a glorious long tail. It is the most versatile thing: Need to traverse a narrow plank, your tail got you covered. Need to pass some time in a wait? Try to catch it, you never will be able to. Or maybe you need to show everyone that you are not in a mood today? Just fluff that bad boy up and they will know better than to mess with you.

  • You will be able to clean yourself fully using the power of your own tongue. No more baths, soaps shampoos and even toilet paper. Just sheer determination and a tool that's always with you.
